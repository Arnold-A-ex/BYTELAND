KYC Badge

Veriff is a highly reputable European company who does 3rd party ID verification, compatible with almost 200 countries. This fast and simple KYC process is handled entirely br Veriff, and at no time will any information provided to them be seen, known or stored by Byteland

Comments on NFT's

When you list an NFT on Byteland, all "Likes" and "Comments" will be transferred with that NFT. When it is sold, the historical data will be transferred with the NFT, and will help in determining the price and seeing the overall popularity of each NFT on the marketplace
